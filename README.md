DermaAI is an AI-powered web application that helps users identify possible skin diseases by analyzing images of affected skin areas. Users can upload or capture images of their skin condition and receive AI-generated predictions along with precautionary advice.

The platform aims to promote early detection of dermatological conditions and guide users toward professional medical consultation when necessary.

Objectives
Provide quick AI-based skin disease prediction
Increase awareness about common skin diseases
Encourage early medical consultation
Improve accessibility to dermatology guidance

Medical Disclaimer

DermaAI is an AI-assisted dermatology support system designed for educational and awareness purposes only.

Predictions generated by the system may not always be accurate. Users should consult a qualified dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
